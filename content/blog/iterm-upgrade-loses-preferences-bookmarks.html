--- title: "iTerm Upgrade Loses Preferences & Bookmarks" date: Thu Aug 21 2008 00:00:00 GMT+1200 (New Zealand Standard Time) ---

UPDATE: iTerm version 0.9.5.0821 has been released which is intended to fix this. I haven't tested it.

I just upgraded to iTerm version 0.9.5.0820 from 0.9.5.0611 on Mac OS X Leopard, and found that iTerm had forgotten or lost all of my preferences and bookmarks.

I fixed it by;

  1. quitting iTerm
  2. going to ~/Library/Preferences/
  3. moving net.sourceforge.iTerm.plist to net.sourceforge.iTerm.plist~
  4. copying iTerm.plist to net.sourceforge.iTerm.plist
  5. starting iTerm
