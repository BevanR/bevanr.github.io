Mac Favicon Deprecated -- And RELEASED!

Firefox 3 Upgrade? Mac Favicon and Mac Favicon XL themes will not be updated for Firefox 3. However, you can restore this feature in Firefox 3 using userstyles.org and the 'Stylish' Fiefox extension. Click your preferred style below, then click 'Load into Stylish' and follow the instructions from userstyles.org; Mac Favicon, Firefox 3: show favicons next to bookmark names/titles Mac Favicon XL, Firefox 3: show favicons only (hide bookmark names/titles).
